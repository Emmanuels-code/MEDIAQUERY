Mickey Mouse is one of the most recognizable and beloved characters in the world. Created in 1928 by Walt Disney and Ub Iwerks, Mickey has become a cultural icon, appearing in countless animated shorts, films, TV shows, and merchandise.

Goofy is a good-natured but slightly dim-witted dog who is one of Mickey Mouse's best friends. He was created in 1932 by Walt Disney and Art Babbit, and has since become a popular character in his own right, appearing in various Disney productions.

Pluto is Mickey Mouse's loyal and faithful pet dog, created in 1930 by Walt Disney and Norm Ferguson. He is a yellow-orange colored pup who is always by Mickey's side, and has become a beloved character in the Disney universe.
